What is BBIT?

BBIT is a hybrid program that was incorporated as a result of the emerging trend of businesses adopting information technologies in their daily operations. This neccesitated the need for IT proffesionals with knowledge in business activities such as accounting, financial management, human resource management among other business activities to study the business needs of business in these fields and come up with programs, softwares and systems to help in the easy running of businesses since most pure IT graduates lacked knowledge in these business fields and could therefore not understand some of the underlying concepts needed to create effective technologies as was required by businesses. An individual pursuing BBIT therefore gets to learn some fundumental concepts of business such as business law, acounting, human resource management among others to develop a deeper understanding of businesses. This does not neccessarily mean that a BBIT graduate does not have understanding of information technologies as this is also covered widely in the course curriculum and one gets to understand concepts such as computer networks, iformation systems and also have knowledge in computer programming with languages such as C, C++, Java being taught with concepts such as object oriented programming. An individual also gets knowledge about the web and how to design one thanks to markup languages such as HTMLbeing included in the curriculum. An individual as gets the knowledge of designing web pages thanks to CSS which is also in the curriculum.
